Anime Recommendation

   BEST MALE ANIME CHARACTER EVER

Name :
      Hikigaya Hachiman (比企谷 八幡)
Birth :
      August 8th (16-17 years old)
Series :
      Yahari Ore no Seishun Rabu Kome wa Machigatteiru! (Oregairu)
Personality :
      He is a cynic, stoic, and a loner. He does things by himself and has never relied on anyone (other than his little sister). As he does not like to rely on others, he has an unusual prideful side. Hachiman refuses to accept anything he considers pity, sympathy, or charity.



Quote

"We only change to avoid the current situation. The true meaning of not running away is planting your feet firmly in the ground and doing the best you can with your present. Who's running away now? Why can't you just accept who you are and who you were?"
